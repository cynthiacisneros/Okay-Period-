About This Site

Okay Period is a small educational site focused on menstrual health literacy. The goal is to offer approachable explanations, practical tips, and pointers to reliable resources—without judgment and without replacing care from a clinician.

Content here is written for general learning. Everyone’s body is different; if you have pain, very heavy bleeding, or other concerns that worry you, talking to a trusted healthcare provider is always a good step.

Okay, period - is for educational purposes. Not medical advice.
