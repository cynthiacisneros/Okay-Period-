Welcome

Hello, and welcome to Okay Period. This site is here to help you understand periods in plain language—what they are, how to feel more comfortable, and where to find trustworthy information and products.

Whether you are new to menstruation, supporting someone who is, or simply want a refresher, you can use the menu at the top to explore topics at your own pace.
