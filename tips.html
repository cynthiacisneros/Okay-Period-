Tips for Period Comfort

Small habits can make a big difference. Here are ideas many people find helpful:

  • Keep supplies you prefer (pads, tampons, cups, period underwear) in your bag or locker.
  • Stay hydrated and eat regular meals; gentle movement or stretching may ease cramps for some.
  • Use a heating pad or warm bath for cramping, if that feels good for your body.
  • Rest when you can; sleep and stress both affect how you feel day to day.
  • Track your cycle in a calendar or app so patterns are easier to notice over time.

Severe pain, very heavy bleeding, or feeling faint are reasons to reach out to a healthcare provider—not something you have to “just put up with.”
