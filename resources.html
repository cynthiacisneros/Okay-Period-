A Little Reassurance ✨

Periods can feel a little scary, especially at first. Your body is changing, and it’s completely okay if that feels like a lot.

You’re learning, and you’ll figure it out in your own time. It won’t always feel this confusing.

Track Your Cycle ✨

You don’t need an app to understand your body.

A simple calendar can help you notice patterns, like when your period starts, how long it lasts, and how you feel from day to day.

Over time, this can help you feel more prepared and a little less surprised.

💡 Most cycles happen about every 21–35 days. Yours may not feel regular at first, and that is completely okay.
