A Little Reassurance ✨

Periods can feel a little scary, especially at first. Your body is changing, and it’s completely okay if that feels like a lot.

You’re learning, and you’ll figure it out in your own time. It won’t always feel this confusing.

Track Your Cycle ✨

You don’t need an app to understand your body.

A simple tracker can help you notice patterns — like when your period starts, how long it lasts, and how you feel.

Over time, this gets easier. And it helps you feel more prepared, not surprised.

💡 Most cycles happen about every 21–35 days. Yours might not be regular at first — that’s completely okay.
