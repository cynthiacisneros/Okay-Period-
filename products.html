Period Products (Overview)

There is no single “right” product—what works best depends on your flow, comfort, activities, and personal preference. Common options include:

  • Disposable pads — worn in underwear; come in many sizes and absorbencies.
  • Tampons — inserted into the vagina to absorb blood; follow package directions and change as recommended.
  • Menstrual cups — reusable cups that collect blood; need cleaning between uses.
  • Period underwear — absorbent fabric built into underwear; washable and reusable.

Always read instructions for any product, especially for first-time use. If you use tampons, know the rare but serious signs of toxic shock syndrome listed on product leaflets and seek care if concerned.
