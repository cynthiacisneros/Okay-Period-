Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a period usually last?

Many periods last about three to seven days, but shorter or slightly longer can still be normal. If your bleeding is very heavy or lasts much longer than usual for you, check in with a healthcare provider.

Is it normal for cycles to be irregular at first?

Yes. It is common for cycles to be uneven for the first few years after periods start. Tracking dates can help you see your own pattern over time.

Can you swim or exercise on your period?

Many people do. Tampons, cups, or period swimwear are options for swimming. Do what feels comfortable and safe for your body.

When should I talk to a doctor?

Reach out if you have severe pain, soak through pads or tampons very quickly, feel dizzy or unusually unwell, or if you have questions about your specific symptoms.
