Real Talk

Let’s talk about the things people don’t always say out loud.

💬 What if I leak?

It happens. To a lot of people. It might feel embarrassing in the moment, but it’s normal. Wearing the right products for your flow and checking in during the day can help, but even then—accidents happen. You’re still okay.

👀 What if someone notices?

Most people are way more focused on themselves than you think. And honestly, periods are a normal part of life. If someone does notice, it’s not something to be ashamed of..

🩹 What if I don’t have supplies?

You’re not stuck. You can ask a friend, a teacher, or the school nurse—people are usually more understanding than you expect. Even using tissue temporarily can help until you get what you need.

🌷 What if I feel weird about all of this?

That makes sense. This is new, and your body is changing. It can feel confusing, uncomfortable, or even frustrating. Give yourself time.
